The Three Types of Diabetes
Type 1
Autoimmune Disease
Body attacks and destroys over 90% of its own inuslin producing beta cells.
Type 2
Lifestyle Dependent
Obesity and lifestyle lead to the development of insulin resistance.
MODY
Genetic Mutation
Single mutation impacts body’s ability to produce insulin.
Diabetes Prevention and Screening
Unlike type 1 diabetes and MODY, type 2 diabetes can be delayed or even prevented with lifestyle changes if caught early enough.

With this in mind, we have created a machine learning model to screen for diabetes by predicting your risk for diabetes based on certain body parameters that have a proven correlation with diabetes.
